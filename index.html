Frequently Asked Questions

What are the care instructions for these Paithani's?

The care instructions for Paithani sarees typically include dry cleaning, gentle handling, avoiding direct contact with perfumes and chemicals, storing in a cool, dry place, and ironing on a low to medium heat setting with caution around metallic threads.

What is the material composition of your sarees?

Paithani sarees are traditionally made from silk, with intricate designs often woven using metallic threads such as gold or silver. Some contemporary variations may incorporate other materials or blends, but silk remains the primary fabric for Paithani sarees due to its luxurious texture and durability.

In what case I can return or exchange sarees?

You can typically return or exchange a saree if it's defective, damaged, incorrect, or if there are size/fit issues. However, policies vary by seller, so review their specific return/exchange policy for details.

How do I contact customer support for further assistance?

Look for contact information such as a phone number or email address.
